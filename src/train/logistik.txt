TERMS AND CRITERIA FOR APPLYING
To apply for halal certifcation, possess a valid Business Registration Certificate from SSM or Cooperative Registration Certificate under SKM or Establishment Document under the Act of Parliament or the respective Ministry and other Government agencies
To apply for halal certifcation, possess a license or approval from the local authority or a valid confirmation letter or letter of support from any Government agency
To apply for halal certifcation, fully operational for at least 3 months prior to applying for SPHM for new applications
To apply for halal certifcation, fully operational for at least 1 month in the new premises for the relocating company
To apply for halal certifcation, manufacture or operate halal products only including trading products and comply with the set halal standards
To apply for halal certifcation, apply for SPHM on all types of products that are still produced at the premises

Non-halal products are not eligible to apply for halal certifcation
Companies that manufacture and/or distribute halal and non-halal products (including food delivery services) are not eligible to apply for halal certifcation
Use of the same brand for halal and non-halal services (subject to application in Malaysia) are not eligible to apply for halal certifcation
Services that do not have standards or guidelines for reference are not eligible to apply for halal certifcation
Use of company names, products and brands that refer to non-halal products or are synonymous with or similar to non-halal products or any misleading terms such as ham, bak kut teh, bacon, beer, rum, hot dog, charsiew and the like are not eligible to apply for halal certifcation
Services that can lead to deviation of faith, superstition and fraud are not eligible to apply for halal certifcation

FEES PAYMENT
For fees payment, each application under the Malaysia Halal Certification (Domestic) is subject to a fee of RM20.00 only.
For fees payment, each application involving foreign companies/applicants conducting product manufacturing within the country is subject to a fee of RM200.00 only.
For fees payment, industry that has an annual income less than RM300,000.00 is considered as micro industry has to pay a fee of RM 200 for new application and renewal
For fees payment, industry that has an annual income from RM300,000.00 to RM 15,000,000.00 is considered as small industry has to pay a fee of RM 800 for new application and renewal
For fees payment, industry that has an annual income from RM 15,000,000.00 to RM 50,000,000.00 is considered as medium industry has to pay a fee of RM 1400 for new application and renewal
For fees payment, industry that has an annual income more than RM50,000,000.00 is considered as large industry has to pay a fee of RM 2000 for new application and renewal
For fees payment, for foreign industry that is the food and drinks, cosmetic and pharmaseutical, used goods and medical devices business in ASEAN has to pay a fee of RM2500 for new application and renewal
For fees payment, for foreign industry that is the food and drinks, cosmetic and pharmaseutical, used goods and medical devices business not in ASEAN has to pay a fee of RM10000 for new application and renewal
For fees payment, MUIS has the authority to exempt fee payments for any applications that are fully managed by any government agency.

PAYMENT METHOD
For halal certification payment method, companies/applicants must make the payment within 14 working days after receiving the charge letter through the MYeHALAL system.
For halal certification payment method, the fee payment must be made via online payment, QR code, or cheque only.
For halal certification payment method, payments should be made payable to Tetuan Majlis Ugama Islam Sabah.
For halal certification payment method, a receipt for each payment made must be issued and submitted to MUIS.
For halal certification payment method, fees paid are non-refundable and non-transferable to other applications.
For halal certification payment method, MUIS reserves the right to impose additional fees if the fee determination information provided by the company/applicant is inaccurate.

The company/applicant that stores dutiable goods must possess the relevant warehousing license from JKDM
The transportation of bulk containers, bulk liquids and general transportation of commercial vehicles used in general haulage and freight, must be registered with JPJ and obtain a permit for the transportation of goods from the relevant agencies under the MOT
Freight forwarding companies or customs agents and shipping agents must obtain the relevant licenses from JKDM
The requirements of MS 2400-1:2019 Halal Supply Chain Management System Part 1 : Transportation-General Requirements (First Revision), MS 2400-2:2019 Halal Supply Chain Management System - Part 2 : Warehousing-General Requirements (First Revision), MS 2400-3:2019 Halal Supply Chain Management System - Part 3 : Retailing-General requirements (First Revision) as well as other latest legislation enforced by the relevant authorities must be complied with
Companies/applicants that carry out storage of meat products and meat products must choose an import permit from JPV
The company/applicant for the retail category must consist of a company that carries out distribution or trading of food products and retailers such as grocery stores, convenience stores and supermarkets
Transportation, warehousing and retail information applied for by SPHM must be declared in detail in the MYeHALAL application form
Companies/applicants who are eligible to apply for this scheme are:
Transportation operators (land, sea and air)
Warehousing entrepreneurs
Retail entrepreneurs

In micro industry, a minimum of 1 Malaysian Muslim employee, holding a permanent position must be appointed and work in the processing area during the operation period or shift
In micro industry, IHCS must be developed and implemented based on the MHMS Manual 2020

In small industry, a minimum of 1 Malaysian Muslim employee, holding a permanent position must be appointed and work in the processing area during the operation period or shift
In small industry, a permanent Halal Supervisor must be appointed at the premises
In small industry, IHCS must be developed and implemented based on the MHMS Manual 2020

In medium and large industry, a minimum of 2 Malaysian Muslim employees, holding permanent positions must be appointed and work in the processing area during the operation period or shift
In medium and large industry, permanent Halal Executives must be appointed at each branch of the premises
In medium and large industry, JKHD shall be established for premises and each branch of the premises has different operational management
In medium and large industry, HAS must be built and implemented based on the MHMS Manual 2020

TRANSPORT
The entire transport network cannot transport and handle any material or product categorised as mughallazah excrement
Halal-certified transportation must transport and handle halal products only
Vehicle tracking systems must be developed and implemented effectively
Halal-certified transportation must be clearly identified and marked
The entire transportation network for food delivery services must be halal certified
Employees, including drivers, are not allowed to bring food or drinks, animals and any other non-halal materials while on duty

WAREHOUSING
The entire premises area cannot store any materials or products categorised as mughallazah excrement
Halal-certified lots must store and handle halal products only
The warehousing management system must be developed and implemented effectively
Halal-certified lots must be identified and physically segregated
Storage for halal-certified ingredients or products and non-halal products (in halal-certified lots) should be established with clear separation and labelling

RETAIL
The entire premises cannot handle, display and sell any material or product categorised as mughallazah najis
Retail applications for the categories of distributors and traders must handle and sell halal materials or products only
Retail applications for the food retailer category must handle, display and sell halal materials or products only in the halal certified section
Food retailers that handle, display and sell non-halal materials or products (except mughallazah) shall have an effective control mechanism covering separate and clearly marked areas
Food retailers that handle, display and sell non-halal materials or products (except mughallazah) shall have an effective control mechanism covering segregated and clearly marked storage
Food retailers that handle, display and sell non-halal materials or products (except mughallazah) shall have an effective control mechanism covering specialized carts or baskets in the section
Food retailers that handle, display and sell non-halal materials or products (except mughallazah) shall have an effective control mechanism covering the dedicated payment counter in the section
Food retailers that handle, display and sell non-halal materials or products (except mughallazah) shall have an effective control mechanism covering operated by non-Muslims only

RENEWAL
SPHM application must be done online through the MYeHALAL system at www.halal.gov.my/v4 
SPHM renewal application must be submitted as early as 6 months and no later than 3 months before the expiry of SPHM 
The SPHM renewal application that has expired must be categorised as a new application 
The Company/applicant must refer to the requirements for filling in the MYeHALAL application form and any update of information related to product name and brand must submit an official notification to MUIS 
Applications that are incomplete and do not follow the correct Malaysian Halal Certification scheme and category will be rejected by MUIS 
Applications will be managed by MUIS based on the Malaysian Halal Certification scheme and the manufacturer's address. 
The company/applicant can find out the officer who manages the application based on the information listed in the user account of the MYeHALAL system 
The company/applicant is not allowed to apply for the change of the competent authority that manages the SPHM application 
Complete supporting documents must be submitted to MUIS (as stated in the applicant's MYeHALAL system) within 5 working days after the MYeHALAL application form is submitted online 
Payment of fees must be cleared within 14 working days upon receipt of the fee payment letter The field audit will be carried out after the fee payment is received by MUIS 

ADDITION
Addition of products in the application is not open for logistics service schemes

MERGER
Any application for merger of SPHM shall be classified as a new application 
Application for merger of SPHM that is still valid or new (if relevant) permitted for food and beverage product schemes 
Application for merger of SPHM shall be submitted within 6 months prior to the expiry of the certification date 
Merger application shall involve all products on SPHM that are still valid 
Application for merger of SPHM shall be accompanied by a letter of accompaniment clearly stating the details prior application 
The procedure for applying for SPHM merger must follow the procedure for SPHM 
New Registration which is still valid must be void if the application for merger is approved by MUIS 
SPHM which has been cancelled must be submitted to MUIS
